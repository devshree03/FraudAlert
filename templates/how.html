How It Works
  1. Ingest the JD via paste, PDF/DOCX, or form.
  2. Preprocess text (clean, tokenize, stem) and create TF‑IDF features.
  3. Append engineered numeric features (e.g., risky phrases count).
  4. Predict with a balanced Logistic Regression and compute probability.
  5. Show label, risk percentage, and top indicative tokens.
