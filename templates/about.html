About

FraudAlert flags risky job postings using transparent ML signals. It supports paste text, PDF/DOCX uploads, and a detailed form, returning a label, risk percentage, and top signals.

FAQ
What counts as a “signal”?

Signals are high‑weight words/phrases in the text (e.g., “registration fee”, “WhatsApp”, “no interview”) that pushed the prediction toward FAKE. They are shown to explain the decision.

Do you store my PDFs or text?

No. Files are processed in a temporary directory and deleted after analysis. Text is handled in memory. You can add opt‑in logging later for diagnostics.

Why did a normal JD show FAKE?

Extraction noise or scam‑like phrasing can spike risk. Check the Signals list, remove unrelated boilerplate, or paste clean text. Thresholds can be tuned to reduce false positives.

Does it work on screenshots?

Only if OCR is enabled (not by default). Text‑based PDFs/DOCX work best.

Is the score a guarantee?

No. It’s a probabilistic risk indicator. Always verify company domain, never pay fees, and avoid off‑platform chats.
